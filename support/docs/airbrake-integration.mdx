Airbrake is a software tool and service designed to assist developers in tracking and monitoring errors within web and mobile applications. Its primary purpose is promptly identifying and diagnosing software errors and exceptions, enabling developers to react swiftly and effectively.TestMu AI's integration with Airbrake provides a seamless solution for tracking and managing bugs and issues encountered during [cross-browser testing](https://stage.testmuinternal.ai/). With this integration, developers can effortlessly log bugs directly from the TestMu AI platform to their Airbrake instance, eliminating the need for manual maintenance of tasks and issues.

## Integrating Airbrake With TestMu AI
***

**Step 1:** Sign in to your TestMu AI account. Don't have an account, [register for free](https://accounts.lambdatest.com/register).

**Step 2:** Select **Integrations** from the left sidebar and head to **Airbrake**.

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/airbrake-install.webp)

**Step 3:** It will route you to the installing console window, where you are required to enter your Airbrake **Auth Token, Project ID, and Project Key**.

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/instance-window.webp)

## Getting The Airbrake Auth Token, Project ID, and Project Key
***

**Step 1:** Login to your **Airbrake** account.

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/airbrake-account.webp)

**Step 2:** From the left sidebar menu, hover on the **profile icon** and click the **Profile and Notifications** tab. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/profile-notifications.webp)

**Step 3:** Copy the **Auth Token**. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/api-token.webp)

To access your **Project ID** and **Key**, head to the **Projects** option in the top-left corner. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/projects.webp)

**Step 1:** Select your preferred project or create a new project. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/select-project.webp)

**Step 2:** Head to the **Settings** tab.

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/settings-tab.webp)

**Step 3:** Copy the **Project ID** and **Project Key**. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/project-id-key.webp)

Copy and paste the required credentials from your Airbrake account and click **Install**. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/click-install.webp)

Now you have successfully integrated your TestMu AI account with Airbrake. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/success-install.webp)

## One-Click Bug Logging With Airbrake
***

**Step 1:** In your TestMu AI Dashboard, head to **Real-Time Testing > Browser Testing** from the left navigation menu.

**Step 2:** To perform **live-interactive testing** of your website or a web app, enter the web URL, select the testing type (Desktop/Mobile), select the browser version, OS, and resolution, and click **Start**.

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/real-time-browser.webp)

Upon clicking, a cloud-based virtual machine will fire up, running real browsers and operating systems where you can test websites and web apps for filing bugs.

**Step 3:** Click on the **Bug icon** from the left in-session toolbar to take a screenshot of any bug you encounter. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/bug-icon.webp)

**Step 4:** After marking the bugs, click on the **Bug** icon in front of **Airbrake** under the integrations section.

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/airbrake-icon.webp)

**Step 5:** In the Airbrak ticket, fill the bug details like **Project, Summary, and Description** and click **Mark As Bug** to log the bugs in Airbrake.

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/airbrake-mark-as-bug.webp)

**Step 6:** To view the logged issues, visit TestMu AI Dashboard and head to **Manage > Issue Tracker** from the left navigation bar.

**Step 7:** Inside the **Issue Tracker**, you'll notice the logged bugs and their test environment details. Click on the **upper right arrow** in the square button to view your logged bugs in Airbrake instance.  

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/issue-tracker.webp)

**Step 8:**  Upon clicking, you will be redirected to the Airbrake dashboard to find your **logged bugs**. 

![add](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/airbrake-instance.webp)

## Uninstalling The Airbrake Integration

***

**Step 1:** Sign in to your TestMu AI account and visit **Integrations** from the left sidebar menu.

**Step 2:** From My Integrations, click **Remove** under the **Airbrake** block.

![remove](/assets/images/airbrake-integration/uninstall-airbrake.webp)
