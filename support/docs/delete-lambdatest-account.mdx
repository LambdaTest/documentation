This documentation will guide you through the steps to delete your <BrandName /> account.


## Deleting Freemium Account

* * *

If you are a <BrandName /> freemium user, you can follow the below steps to delete your account.

**Step 1**: Login to your <BrandName /> account. Navigate to the upper-right corner and click on **'Profile'**.

![Profile](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/dashboard.webp)

**Step 2**: From the dropdown, click on **Account Settings**.

![Delete account](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/account-settings.webp)

**Step 3:** To delete your <BrandName /> account, head to the **Click Here** option.

![Email Address](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/click-here.webp)

**Step 4**: An email notification will be sent to your registered e-mail address. An email will consist of the further instruction you need to follow for deleting your <BrandName /> account.

![Email Address](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/e-mail-.webp)

Read the instructions and reply as stated in the e-mail.

<Warning>
I understand that the deletion is irrecoverable and I wish to delete my <BrandName /> account.
</Warning>

**Step 5**: Once your request is processed, your <BrandName /> account will be deleted.

## Deleting Premium Account

* * *

If you are a premium user and wish to delete your account, you will need to cancel your current plan subscription. Once your plan gets expired, you can follow the above-mentioned steps to delete your account. For canceling the current plan subscription, refer to [Manage Subscriptions](/support/docs/manage-subscriptions/).

## Deleting Premium Account Of A Team Member

* * *

If you are a member of the <BrandName /> organization's team, you will need to seek permission from the team's administrator to remove you from the team. Once removed, you can follow the steps mention in _Deleting Premium Account_ to delete your account.

### Removing Member From The Team

* * *

As an admin, you can remove the members from an organization.

**Step 1**: Login to your <BrandName /> account. Navigate to the upper-right corner and click on **'Profile'**.

![Profile](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/dashboard.webp)

**Step 2**: From the dropdown, click on **Account Settings**.

![manage team](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/org-settings.webp)

**Step 3**: Navigate to the user that you want to delete. Click on the **three dots** next to the user.

![Delete account](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/three-dots.webp)

**Step 4**: From the dropdown, Click on **'Delete'**. 

![Delete account](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/delete-user.webp) 

**Step 5**: A popup box will appear, Click on **'Delete'**. 

![Delete account](/assets/images/delete-lambdatest-account/confirm-delete.webp) The user will be removed successfully.
