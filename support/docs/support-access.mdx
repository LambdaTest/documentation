The Support Access feature allows <BrandName /> to take access to your <BrandName /> account and log in on your behalf from the backend. By granting access to your account, our team can help you with debugging issues or do activities on your behalf. You can revoke this access anytime

<Tip>
**Quick Link**

You can directly navigate to the Support Access page by this [**link**](https://accounts.lambdatest.com/support).
</Tip>
## Granting Support Access
***

**Step 1:** Sign in to your <BrandName /> account. Don't have an account, [register for free](https://accounts.lambdatest.com/register).

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/support-access/lambdatest-dashboard.webp' alt="add" width="1346" height="647" />

**Step 2:**  Click on **Profile Icon** and go to **Organization Settings** as shown in the image below. 

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/support-access/organization-settings.webp' alt="add" width="1346" height="647" />

**Step 3:** Head to the **Support Access** tab and click on **Grant Access**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/support-access/support-access.webp' alt="add" width="1346" height="647" />

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/support-access/granted-access.webp' alt="add" width="1346" height="647" />

You have now successfully granted support access to <BrandName />. 

## Revoking Support Access
***

* Simply click on Revoke <BrandName /> Access. 

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/support-access/revoke-access.webp' alt="add" width="1346" height="647" />
