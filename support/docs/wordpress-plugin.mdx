# WordPress Plugin

* * *

>With <BrandName /> plugin for WordPress, you can generate automated screenshots of your web pages and post directly from your WordPress platform.

## How To Install & Use <BrandName /> WordPress Plugin?
* * *

**Step 1:** Login to your WordPress account.

**Step 2:** Goto plugins and click on **Add New** plugin button.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/1.webp' alt="Adding New Plugin in Wordpress" width="1191" height="655" />

**Step 3:** Search for **<BrandName />** keyword in search bar and click on **install** button.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/2.webp' alt="installing <BrandName /> plugin" width="1366" height="636" />

**Step 4:** Click on **Activate** button and you are all set with <BrandName /> plugin.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/3.webp' alt="activate <BrandName /> Screenshot Plugin" width="1366" height="636" />

**Step 5:** Now you can see **<BrandName /> Screenshot** in plugin option on your WordPress platform.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/4.webp' alt="<BrandName /> screenshot plugin" width="1177" height="652" />

**Step 6:** Now go to <BrandName /> plugin and add <BrandName /> profile account details.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/5.webp' alt="access key for screenshot testing" width="1366" height="661" />

**Step 7:** Go to profile section on your <BrandName /> account and copy access key.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/6.webp' alt="<BrandName /> User Profile" width="1361" height="663" />

**Step 8:** Add Email address and paste Access key in your WordPress platform. Click on Activate button.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/7.webp' alt="activate screenshot testig feature" width="1362" height="661" />

**Step 9:** You are all set with WordPress plugin.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/8.webp' alt="screenshot testing setup" width="1356" height="660" />

**Step 10:** Now you can Generate [Automated Screenshots](https://www.lambdatest.com/automated-screenshot) of your web pages or posts with <BrandName /> plugin. Go to All Posts and you will <BrandName /> icon below every post. Click on that icon.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/9.webp' alt="Generate Automated Screenshots" width="1366" height="660" />

**Step 11:** Now select the browser, browser version and mobile devices where you want to take screenshots and hit **Start** button.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/10.webp' alt="take screenshots" width="1366" height="660" />

**Step 12:** Here all the screenshots are generated.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/wordpress-plugin/11.webp' alt="generated automated screenshots" width="1366" height="663" />
