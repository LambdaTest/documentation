It's easy to manage your subscriptions by upgrading, removing, and switching the current plan. For example - upgrading from a free plan to a premium plan, removing an existing plan or switch from a monthly plan to an annual plan.

## Upgrading From Free Plan
* * *

1. Login to your <BrandName /> account.

2. Click **Upgrade** on the top right corner of the dashboard.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/upgrade.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

3. From the left-flexbox, choose the plan as per your choice and requirement. Then click **GET STARTED**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/select-plan.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

4. It will redirect you to the checkout page. Verify the details and then select **PROCEED TO CHECKOUT**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/checkout.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

>*In case you wish add more products, click **Add More Products**. Each product has a dropdown below QTY category through which you can customize your parallel test plans.*

5. Enter the payment details, billing address, and info. Click **MAKE PAYMENT**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/payment.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

After few moments, a confirmation screen will appear stating the final status of your opted plan.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/final.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

## Customizing Plans
* * *
You can customize your plans by adding new plans or remove existing ones. Choose any of them as per your choice.

### Adding A New Plan
***

1. Login to your <BrandName /> account.

2. Click **Upgrade** on the top right corner of the dashboard.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/upgrade.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

3. Select **View Subscription**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/view-subs.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

4. Click **Go to Subscriptions & Billings** .

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/goto.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

5. Under the Subscriptions section, click **Edit**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/pencil.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

6. It will route you to the checkout page. From the **Select Product** category, expand the dropdown and choose a new plan.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/new-plan.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

7. After selecting the plan, verify the details and click **PROCEED TO CHECKOUT**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/checkout-new.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

8. The price summary of the selected plan will show up. Click **MAKE PAYMENT**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/mkp.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

After few moments, a confirmation screen will appear stating the final status of your additional plan.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/final1.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

### Removing An Existing Plan
***

Follow the steps 1 to 5 mentioned for *Adding New Plan*. 

6. Once you are in checkout page, Click 'x' on the plan that you wish to remove.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/cross.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

7. After removing the plan, verify the details and click **PROCEED TO CHECKOUT**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/checkout3.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

8. The price summary of the removed plan will show up. Click **CONFIRM**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/confirm.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

After few moments, a confirmation screen will appear stating the final status of your removed plan.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/final2.webp' alt="Upgrade Button" width="768" height="614" />

### Switching From Monthly To Annual Plan
* * *

If you are using a Monthly plan, you can always switch to Annual Plan and save up to 20%. 

1. Login to your <BrandName /> account.

2. Click **Upgrade** on the top right corner of the dashboard.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/upgrade.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

3. Now enable the toggle button from Monthly to Yearly at the top-right.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/toggle.webp' alt="Pricing Page" width="768" height="599" />

## Canceling The Current Plan Subscription
* * *
**Step 1**: Login to your <BrandName /> account.

**Step 2**: Navigate to the profile avatar at the top-right corner. Click on **‘Billing’**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/Screenshot-219.webp' alt="manage subscription" width="930" height="409" />

**Step 3**: Scroll down and click on **‘CANCEL SUBSCRIPTION’**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/Screenshot-220.webp' alt="manage subscription" width="930" height="405" />

**Step 4**: Select the reason for canceling subscription from the given options. You can also mention the reason for canceling the subscription in the the description box. After that select **‘Complete Cancellation’**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/Screenshot-221.webp' alt="manage subscription" width="930" height="419" />

You’ll be redirected to a page where you’ll notice a message as highlighted below.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/image.webp' alt="manage subscription" width="930" height="443" />

In case you wish to resume your current plan, you do so by clicking on **‘Resume your subscription’**.

<img loading="lazy" src='/assets/images/manage-subscriptions/resume.webp' alt="manage subscription" width="930" height="203" />

That's it! You can now successfully upgrade to a new plan, switch between plans, and switch from Monthly to Annual plans.
